# Viewing POCs

Welcome! As a customer, you've been invited to participate in a Proof of Concept (POC) engagement. This guide will help you understand how to view and navigate your POC.

## Getting Started

### Your Invitation

You received an email invitation containing:

- POC name and description
- Details about the engagement
- A unique access link
- Instructions to get started

### First-Time Access

1. Click the link in your invitation email
2. If you don't have an account:
   - You'll be prompted to create a password
   - Fill in your profile information
   - Click **Create Account**
3. If you already have an account:
   - Log in with your credentials
4. You'll be taken directly to your POC

## Filters and Views

### Filtering Tasks

Filter tasks by:

- **Status**: Show only In Progress, Completed, etc.
- **Assigned To**: Your tasks vs team tasks
- **Task Group**: View specific phases

### Sorting Tasks

Sort tasks by:

- **Order**: Default logical order
- **Status**: Group by completion state
- **Date**: By due date or creation date

### View Options

Choose your preferred view:

- **List View**: Detailed task list
- **Kanban View**: Visual board by status
- **Timeline View**: Calendar-based view

## FAQs

**Q: Can I edit task information?**  
A: No, only the POC team can edit tasks. You can add comments and mark tasks complete.

**Q: Why can't I see some comments?**  
A: Internal team comments are not visible to customers for team coordination.

**Q: Can I add other people from my team?**  
A: No, ask your Sales Engineer to send them an invitation.

**Q: How do I know if I need to take action?**  
A: Tasks assigned to you or marked "Needs Customer Input" require your attention.

**Q: Can I download the POC information?**  
A: Yes, you can generate reports in PDF or Markdown format.

**Q: What happens after the POC ends?**  
A: You'll retain read-only access to the POC for reference.
