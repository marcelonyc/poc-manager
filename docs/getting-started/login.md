# Login & Authentication

## Accessing POC Manager

### Login Page

1. Navigate to your POC Manager URL (provided by your administrator)
2. You'll see the login page with:
   - **Email address** field
   - **Password** field
   - **Sign in** button

### Signing In

!!! example "Login Steps"
    1. Enter your email address
    2. Enter your password
    3. Click **Sign in**
    4. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### First Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

1. You should have received an invitation email with your credentials
2. Use the temporary password provided
3. After first login, you may be prompted to change your password

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### Incorrect Credentials

!!! warning "Login Failed"
    If you see "Login failed" or "Invalid credentials":
    
    - Double-check your email address for typos
    - Ensure CAPS LOCK is not enabled
    - Verify you're using the correct password
    - Contact your tenant administrator if issues persist

### Account Not Active

If your account is not active:

- Contact your tenant administrator
- They may need to activate your account
- Check your email for any activation links

### Forgotten Password

!!! info "Password Recovery"
    Currently, password resets must be handled by:
    
    - Your tenant administrator (for regular users)
    - Platform administrator (for tenant admins)
    
    Contact them to reset your password.

## Security Best Practices

### Strong Passwords

Create a strong password that:

- Is at least 8 characters long
- Contains uppercase and lowercase letters
- Includes numbers and special characters
- Is unique to POC Manager (don't reuse passwords)

### Session Management

- Your session remains active while you're using the application
- You'll be automatically logged out after extended inactivity
- Always log out when using shared computers

### Browser Compatibility

POC Manager works best with modern browsers:

- ✅ Chrome (latest version)
- ✅ Firefox (latest version)
- ✅ Safari (latest version)
- ✅ Edge (latest version)
